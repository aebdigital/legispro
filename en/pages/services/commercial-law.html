Commercial Law Services

Debt Collection

We have years of experience in debt collection for our clients within the territory of the Slovak Republic and the European Union. The success of debt recovery also depends on immediate and intensive communication with the debtor. In such cases, the debtor often agrees to one of our proposed and proven methods of debt repayment.

Company Formation

We will assess your business model, propose the most suitable type of legal form, and professionally establish your new company.

Company Liquidation

We will help you with ending your business and take over communication with your accountant so that your company is deleted from the Commercial Register in the shortest possible time.

Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)

Do you do business under multiple companies that have the same business object? Do we need to optimize your business model to avoid potential penalties from the financial administration? We will prepare a merger project for your companies and perform all necessary actions to avoid potential risks.

Sale of Enterprise or its Part

Do you need to sell your enterprise or part of it? Do not hesitate to contact us.

Filing Bankruptcy Petitions

Are you a creditor and have a debtor who is not paying your receivables? We will verify the possibility of filing a bankruptcy petition against your debtor, or we will register your claims and represent you throughout the process.

Are you a debtor and need to find out if you have an obligation to declare bankruptcy? We will analyze your liabilities, help you file a bankruptcy petition, and guide you through the entire process.

Registration in the Register of Public Sector Partners

Do you need to register as a public sector partner? As an authorized person, we will register you and carry out verification at regular intervals.

Registration of Shareholders List (CDCP)

We have extensive experience with joint-stock companies. We are in regular contact with the Central Securities Depository. We will represent you throughout the entire process from the conclusion of the Agreement on Keeping the List of Shareholders to the registration of the list of shareholders in the Central Securities Depository.
